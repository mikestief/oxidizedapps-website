About

Oxidized Apps, LLC is a software development studio dedicated to building digital tools for the physical world. Our flagship product, Rusty Maintenance Man, is a rugged, intuitive tracking app designed to help owners manage the upkeep of their vehicles and properties. We believe that software should be as dependable as a well-maintained engine, helping users prevent rust and wear before it starts.
